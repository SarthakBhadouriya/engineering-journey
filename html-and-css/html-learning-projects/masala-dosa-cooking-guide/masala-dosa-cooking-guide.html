Masala Dosa :3

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making MASALA DOSA!! Masala Dosa is one of South India's most beloved dishes, known for its crispy golden crepe and flavorful spiced potato filling. In this guide, you'll learn how to prepare an authentic masala dosa from scratch, along with tips to achieve the perfect texture and taste at home.

Ingredients

  • Parboiled rice
  • Urad dal (black gram)
  • Potato (for the filling)
  • Onion
  • Mustard seeds
  • Green chilli
  • Chana dal
  • Rice flour

Instructions

  1. Soak the ingredients
    • Soak 3 cups rice and 1 cup urad dal separately for 4–6 hours.
    • Soak 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds with the dal.
  2. Grind the batter
    • Grind the urad dal and fenugreek into a smooth paste.
    • Grind the rice into a slightly coarse paste.
  3. Mix and ferment
    • Combine both pastes and add salt.
    • Mix well and leave the batter to ferment for 8-12 hours or overnight.
  4. Prepare the potato masala
    • Boil and mash 4–5 potatoes.
    • Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, sliced onions, and turmeric.
    • Add the mashed potatoes and mix well. Set aside.
  5. Heat the dosa pan
    • Heat a flat griddle (tawa) on medium heat.
    • Lightly grease it with oil.
  6. Spread the batter
    • Pour a ladle of batter onto the center of the tawa.
    • Spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  7. Cook the dosa
    • Drizzle a little oil or ghee around the edges.
    • Cook until the bottom turns golden brown and crisp.
  8. Add the masala filling
    • Place a portion of the potato masala in the center of the dosa.
  9. Fold the dosa
    • Fold the dosa in half or roll it around the filling.
  10. Serve hot
    • Serve with coconut chutney and sambar.
